Calculating Solar Power

Solar power may only be used within and including the orbit of Jupiter. Calculate the area in square meters of the solar panels using:

solar panel area (m2) = 0.015*a2*Prequired

where a is the semi-major axis of the target around the Sun in AU and Prequired is the power required by the spacecraft in Watts.

Solar Panels also require the use of a battery to store and supply energy during the night.
