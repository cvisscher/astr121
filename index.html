ASTR 121 Mission Planning Overview

Identifying Mission Goals

Your task is to design a mission to answer NASA's burning questions about the solar system. Take some time to select a target of interest in the solar system. This could be a planet, a moon, an asteroid, etc. After selecting your target, identify some priority questions to answer. Use the decadal survey published by NASA to help identify these questions.
